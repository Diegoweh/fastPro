Who We Are

Who We Are Headquartered out of Richardson, TX and a division of United Bags, BAG Corp is a bulk handling and supply chain solutions provider structured with one mission in mind: Helping businesses manage all of their bulk handling needs from one source. BAG Corp's manufacturing resources for the SUPER SACK® container and related FIBC products include our North American manufacturing operation located in Mexico in combination with our international manufacturing and warehousing partners who ensure all our FIBCs meet the tough BAG Corp specification and performance standards.

What We Do

What We Do BAG Corp is a trusted advisor to clients first and foremost. It is through getting to know our clients specific need, we are able to help them meet their supply chain goals. These goals are met through our advisory services, engineering services, technical support, supply chain and logistics management. BAG Corp ensures your product is of the upmost quality, and with our virtually limitless global production capacity, we can provide bulk bags in a timely, cost-effective manner. In addition, we stock over 360,000 FIBCs for immediate delivery.
