Services

Our Services

FASTPRO is a trusted advisor to clients first and foremost. It is through getting to know our clients specific need, we are able to help them meet their supply chain goals.

Engineering

Our team of experts will develop custom bulk packaging and other innovative products for your unique need.

Technical Services

An invaluable resource, the Technical Service Group is available to aid FIBC users with their processing and packaging operations.

Logistics

Our logistics group will be happy to work with you to expedite your shipment for complex delivery schedules for your company's FIBC needs around the world.

Stocking

FASTPRO maintains a network of stocking warehouses throughout the United States and internationally, that house over 300,000 off-the-shelf, in-stock SUPER SACK® containers.

Quality Testing

Our test facility allows us to conduct FIBC testing that includes drop impact, stacking, tear, top lift, topple, and righting, such as that specified by UN, DOT, NEL, British Standard, and ISO.

Sampling

Sampling of stock or custom FIBCs is available when a new application is addressed or a design is developed. Lead-time for samples is typically 5 to 15 days.

Super Strength

Super Strength With its strength, versatility and large selection of design options, the SUPER SACK® container is ideal for the shipping, handling and storing of dry, flowable products for a wide range of industries. SUPER SACK® containers can be used for products ranging from adhesives, chemicals and paints to building and landscape materials, cement, paving materials, sand, stone, soil and clay to food products including rice, beans, grains, sugar, salt and meat.
